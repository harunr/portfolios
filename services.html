Application Performance Monitoring

AppDynamics’ suite of application and business monitoring solutions allows you to automate anomaly detection and drastically reduce Mean Time to Resolution by correlating application performance to expected baselines. Leverage machine learning through our Cognition Engine, even in multi-cloud environments, to capture business performance metrics and data for root cause remediation. 

Business IQ

Compare business metrics to application performance

AppDynamics Business iQ takes code-level monitoring insights and translates them into clear, actionable insights by monitoring application performance alongside user experiences and matching them to business outcomes. An industry first, Business iQ gives your business and IT departments full-stack performance monitoring and automatic insights into your application and business performance.
