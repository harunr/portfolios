About us

Our Story

Established in 1992, we have witnessed the drastic change in technology as well as the need for technology. We have always had to adapt to the exponential changes as we now find ourselves at the bleeding edge once again.

Vision

IT-tricker will continue to be innovative and strive to offer quality specialist services around the Gartner leading technology that we represent in sub-Saharan Africa. We take pride in saying that we are the number 1 application quality testing, diagnosis and monitoring technology companies across the continent.

Technology

We represent leading products and are the partners of choice for Perfecto Mobile, Appdynamics and Microfocus. We provide on-site and off-site support for our products. We also offer full service test resourcing to cover all testing needs as well as turnkey testing strategy auditing.
